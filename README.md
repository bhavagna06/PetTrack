# PetTrack
PetTrack: Reuniting Lost Pets with Their Families
PetTrack is a mobile app dedicated to helping pet owners reunite with their lost cats and dogs. Whether you're a worried pet parent or a kind-hearted finder, PetTrack makes it easier and faster to report, discover, and connect.

Key Features:
Lost Pet Reporting

Owners can post detailed information including photos, breed, collar color, and last known location.

Posts are visible to the community, increasing the chances of recovery.

Found Pet Submissions

Finders can report rescued or sighted pets with photos and optional GPS tagging.

Helps locate the owner quickly through shared details and location.

Smart Matching System

Suggests potential matches between lost and found pets based on visual similarities and geographic proximity.

Reduces manual searching effort and speeds up reunification.

QR Tag Scanning

Supports scanning of QR-coded digital tags worn by pets for immediate identification.

Displays owner's contact info if available, ensuring faster contact.

How it Works:
Owners or finders upload pet information through a simple and clean interface.

The app uses image and location-based logic to find matches.

Notifications are sent when a possible match is found.

Benefits:
Community-Driven – Every user contributes to helping others, building a trusted network.

Faster Pet Recovery – Increases the speed and success rate of reuniting pets with owners.

Ease of Use – Designed for non-technical users with a focus on simplicity and clarity.

GPS & QR Support – Modern tracking and scanning capabilities enhance functionality.

Potential Extensions:
Shelter and vet clinic integrations.

Lost pet alert system in nearby areas.

AI-based photo matching for enhanced accuracy.

Summary:
PetTrack bridges the gap between lost and found pets using technology and community support. With features like photo sharing, GPS tagging, and QR scanning, it streamlines the search and recovery process — giving pet owners peace of mind and increasing the chances of happy reunions.
